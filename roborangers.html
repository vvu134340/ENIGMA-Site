ABOUT US

THE STORY

Building the next generation of Innovators.

We are RoboRangers, a robotics team from Moldova, founded in 2015, passionate about innovation and technology. In recent years, we have successfully represented our country at international competitions, winning great awards.

Over the years, RoboRangers has become more than just a robotics team—it has grown into a family. For every member, competition preparations are not an obligation but a way of life.

OUR MISSION

To bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application through competitive robotics.

Our Journey Through Time

2015

2015

Team Foundation

RoboRangers was founded with a vision to inspire young minds in robotics and technology. Our journey began with a small group of passionate students and dedicated mentors, setting the foundation for future achievements in Moldova.

2017

2017

FLL National Champions

RoboRangers won the FIRST LEGO League Moldova National Championship for the first time. This achievement marked a new milestone in our journey and showcased our dedication to excellence.

2019

2019

National Champions

A breakthrough year for RoboRangers as we won our first FIRST LEGO League Moldova National Championship. This victory marked the beginning of our competitive success story and established us as a dedicated robotics team in Moldova.

2022

2022

International Success

Our team members represented Moldova at FGC 2022 in Athens, achieving remarkable success and bringing home gold medals. This achievement put Moldova on the global robotics map and inspired future generations to pursue STEM.

2023

2023

FLL National Championship

RoboRangers won the FIRST LEGO League Moldova National Championship for the third time. This achievement marked a new milestone in our journey and showcased our dedication to excellence.

2024

2024

FLL Florida Invitational

A landmark year featuring outstanding achievements at the FIRST LEGO League Florida Invitational in Daytona Beach, securing 3rd place in Robot Performance, Finalist Award, and Mentor Award among 1,500 global participants. We also won the National Championship for the third time and continued our success at FGC 2024.
