About Us

Creating technology that makes a difference

We are ENIGMA, a robotics team from the Republic of Moldova, founded in 2019. Through passion, dedication, and continuous growth, we have evolved into one of the leading robotics teams in our country.

What defines us the most is our mindset: we do not stop halfway. We are committed to finishing what we start and creating something meaningful that can make a real impact.

Because for us, robotics is not just about robots - it is about building the future.

Meet our team

Our mission

Our mission is to promote the FIRST Core Values, inspire innovation and teamwork through robotics, and actively contribute to our community through volunteering and outreach. We strive to empower young minds and help build a generation of individuals who will become the pillars of the future and create meaningful change in society.
