FIRST AGE 2025-2026

Competitions

Our journey through the 2025-2026 FIRST season, competing at regional, national, and international levels.

FEATURED

First Tech Challenge - National Championship

February 21-22, 2026

Between February 21 and 22, 2026, we participated in the FTC Moldova National Championship, the most important robotics competition at national level. The event brought together 31 of the top teams from across the country, creating a highly competitive environment focused on innovation, technical excellence, and strategic gameplay.

During the qualification matches, we demonstrated consistent performance, securing multiple victories and maintaining a strong position in the rankings (2nd place). Our success was driven by a well-coordinated strategy, efficient mechanisms, and strong teamwork.

In the elimination stage, we formed a strong alliance with team CaiVerziPePereți (#28133) and competed in high-intensity matches, showcasing our ability to adapt under pressure and collaborate effectively with partner teams.

In the end, we became the Captain of the Winning Alliance, demonstrating strong leadership, strategic thinking, and effective collaboration throughout the elimination rounds. Our performance was further recognized with the Think Award (1st Place), highlighting the quality of our engineering design process and technical excellence.

Following these achievements, we successfully qualified for the European Premier Event in the Netherlands, where we have the opportunity to compete at an international level alongside some of the best teams in the world.

FIRST LEGO League Moldova – Semifinal & Final

March 14 & 22, 2026

During March 2026, we participated in both the Semifinal and Final stages of FIRST LEGO League Moldova, a competition focused on innovation, research, and robotics fundamentals.

In the semifinal stage, we presented our innovation project, robot design, and core values, successfully advancing to the final round. Our preparation and teamwork allowed us to stand out in multiple evaluation areas and to win The Robot Performance Award.

At the final stage, we demonstrated significant progress, refining both our robot performance and presentation skills. The experience highlighted our ability to combine technical knowledge with creativity and effective communication, also winning The Robot Performance Award.

Central Regional Competition Romania

February 28 - March 1, 2026

Between February 28 and March 1, 2026, we participated in the Central Regional FTC Competition in Alba Iulia, Romania, an international-level event that brought together highly competitive teams from across the region.

On the first day, we completed the technical inspections and judging interviews, where we presented our robot design, programming strategies, and outreach initiatives. This stage emphasized both our technical capabilities and our team's overall vision.

Throughout the qualification matches, we achieved strong and consistent results, successfully adapting to different alliance partners and gameplay situations. Our robot's reliability and strategic execution allowed us to remain competitive against experienced teams.

The competition provided us with valuable international experience, strengthening our ability to perform in diverse and high-pressure environments.
