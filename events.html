Season Events

From our bold workshops to strategic partner collaborations, we amplify robotics and STEM across our region.

02 April 2026

Journey to Space "Jeff Williams Edition"

A bold demonstration honoring astronaut Jeffrey Williams, where teams united to inspire learners and showcase our robotics innovation.

As part of Journey to Space – Jeff Williams Edition, we joined forces with other robotics teams to launch a powerful demonstration of STEM innovation and teamwork.

The event connected us with the broader engineering and STEM community, letting us share ideas, spark curiosity, and showcase our team’s innovation.

05 February 2026

Hour of LEGO

On World LEGO Day, our team led an immersive robotics workshop that challenged students with FIRST-style missions, creative builds, and real teamwork.

Participants tackled BUILD, SUMO, and EXPLORE challenges by constructing LEGO models, programming robots, and testing ideas in a fast-paced learning environment.

14 December 2025

Hour of Code

A high-energy coding workshop where young learners discovered robotics through hands-on LEGO and electronics.

Our mentor and team members led participants through Spike Prime and Adafruit sessions, building confidence and real technical skills.

This event was a powerful example of our commitment to mentorship, teamwork, and preparing young creators for the future.

29-30 November 2025

Rise Up! Moldova

Organized with RoSophia, this two-day training camp sharpened FTC strategy, coding, and competitive teamwork for the season ahead.

We analyzed game strategy, developed robot models, and sharpened competition planning while also covering awards, notebook preparation, and team leadership.

The event strengthened our collaboration and gave participants the practical tools they need to compete with confidence.

11 September 2025

“Tech Explorers” Workshop

A three-day STEAM bootcamp that introduced students to robotics, FIRST programs, and real-world technology skills.

Participants built and programmed Ozobots, explored FIRST programs, and worked with an FTC robot to bring ideas to life.

The workshop connected students to STEAM and showcased how our team turns curiosity into creative, hands-on learning.

15 August 2025

Teachers Forum — Educational Workshop

We empowered educators with hands-on robotics training, from SumoBot builds to FIRST LEGO League Explore strategy.

Our team delivered practical robotics workshops, helping teachers master SumoBot construction and FLL Explore lesson planning.

The experience gave educators the confidence to bring robotics into classrooms and expand STEM learning for students.
